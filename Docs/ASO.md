## Description
```
ToneVault is the fastest way to save and recall the exact knob positions of your guitar pedals and amps — so you never lose a tone after tweaking.

Instead of scanning photos with a flaky camera, you build each pedal or amp by hand: pick a control layout, then drag the virtual knobs, faders, and switches to match your real hardware. Saving is instant. Cloning a tone to make a variant is one tap.

WHY GUITARISTS SWITCH TO TONEVAULT
• 100% offline. No account, no login, no cloud. Works in a windowless studio in airplane mode.
• Manual, tactile knob setting — no camera, no AI scanning.
• Organize tones by SONG, then group songs into SETLISTS for a gig.
• Stage view: big, high-contrast settings you can read in a dark venue.
• Your data is yours: one-tap backup and export, always free.
• One-time purchase. No subscription, ever.

BUILT FOR REAL RIGS
• Generic control templates: 3–6 knob pedals, amp head (gain/bass/mid/treble/presence/master), 3-band EQ, 5/7/10-band graphic EQ, rotary switches, toggle rows, mini-pot rows.
• Attach a photo of the pedal and (Pro) a short audio clip of the tone.
• Show knob values as numbers (0–10) or clock positions — your choice.

FREE + PRO
Free includes up to 5 gear and 10 saved tones, with full backup and export. ToneVault Pro is a one-time $5.99 unlock for unlimited gear, tones, songs and setlists, audio clip attachments, and printable PDF rig cheat-sheets.

Not affiliated with or endorsed by any pedal or amplifier manufacturer. All gear names are entered by you.
```
